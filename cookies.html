1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website.

They are commonly used to help websites work properly, remember preferences, improve security, and provide information to website owners about how the website is used.

2. How We Use Cookies

The Deep Chill website currently uses only cookies that are necessary for the website to function properly.

These are known as essential cookies or strictly necessary cookies.

We do not currently use:

  • Advertising cookies
  • Tracking cookies
  • Marketing cookies
  • Meta Pixel
  • Google Analytics or other third-party analytics cookies

If we introduce analytics, advertising, tracking, or other non-essential cookies in the future, we will update this Cookie Policy and, where required, ask for your consent before placing those cookies on your device.

3. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are needed for the website to work correctly and securely.

These cookies may be used to:

  • Keep the website functioning properly
  • Maintain your browsing session
  • Protect the website from security risks
  • Remember basic settings needed for the site to work
  • Allow enquiry forms to function correctly

Essential cookies cannot usually be switched off through the website, because the website may not work properly without them.

No personal information is stored in these cookies beyond what is technically necessary for the website to function.

4. Third-Party Services

The Deep Chill website may use third-party services to help the website function, display correctly, and process enquiries.

These may include:

  • Website hosting services
  • Enquiry or contact form services
  • Email services
  • Payment providers, where you follow a payment or Direct Debit setup link

If you complete an enquiry form, contact us, or set up a Direct Debit, your personal information will be handled as described in our Privacy Policy.

Payment and Direct Debit setup may be handled by a secure third-party payment provider, such as GoCardless. Any cookies or tracking used by that provider when you visit their website or payment page will be covered by their own privacy and cookie policies.

5. Google Fonts and Website Design

The website may use fonts, images, scripts, or design tools provided by third-party services.

Where possible, we aim to keep the website simple and avoid unnecessary tracking.

If Google Fonts or other externally loaded design tools are used, technical information such as your IP address may be shared with the relevant provider when the website loads.

6. Analytics and Advertising Cookies

We do not currently use analytics or advertising cookies.

If we add tools such as Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Google Ads, Facebook Ads tracking, or similar technologies in the future, we will update this Cookie Policy.

Where required, we will ask for your consent before using non-essential cookies.

7. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored
  • Delete cookies
  • Block some or all cookies
  • Set preferences for particular websites

Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect how the Deep Chill website works.

8. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date.
